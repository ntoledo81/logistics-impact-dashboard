Key snapshot: Of 809 schools in the analytical view, 449 (55.5%) have received at least one delivery and show an 87.3% active rate. Another 312 schools have no order in the system and 48 are awaiting delivery.
2 Monthly Cohort Timeline
Dec 2025 cohort dominates: With 192 schools, the December cohort accounts for the largest onboarding wave. The Jan–Mar 2026 cohorts show steadily lower school counts but comparable class/student averages — suggesting a healthy late-onboarding tail.
3 Delivery Timing vs Engagement
Earlier deliveries = higher engagement: Schools receiving devices before the holiday break (≤ Dec 19) and during holidays show the highest active rates (89–92%) and completion rates (83–86%). Late deliveries (after Feb 16) drop to 72.7% active. Pending/No-Order groups show dramatically lower engagement.
4 Weekly Onboarding Granularity
W01/2026 spike: Week 1 of 2026 (Jan 2–8) shows the highest average students per school (430.3) driven by 9 large schools. W52 and W50 of 2025 also show strong numbers, confirming that early adopters tend to be larger institutions.
